    HWA - 2_Tribe of Hackers Discussion

    66 66 unread replies. 107 107 replies.

    Write a discussion post based on the Tribe of Hackers reading: 

    1. Pick three hackers from the list of seventy.

    2. Read the three bios, then pick one to focus your discussion on. Research your main person’s career, social media, research, etc.

    3. In your opening paragraph tell me which three hackers you read about and why you’re focusing on your main choice.

    4. In the next two paragraphs, tell me something about the two you didn’t choose.

    5. For the rest of the post, excluding the concluding paragraph, tell me what you’ve found out in your additional research about your main choice, as well as why you decided to continue with the choice 

    6. In your concluding paragraph(s), tell me why you choose the person you did and if your research about the person persuaded you to consider doing what they do as a career.

    This assignment has three parts:

    1. Your initial post;

    2. You must have at least three (3) reactions-responses from at least three (3) classmates (3 different people);

    3. You must respond to at least three (3) other posts of your classmates (3 different people).

    The assignment is due 10/04/2020, 11:59 PM.

    Each reaction/response = 0.3%. For full credit, you must have 3 reactions (from 3 different people) and respond to 3 people.

     

    NOTE: Your initial post should be a Reply to the assignment, but the reactions should be a Reply to your classmate's post!

     

    Rubric:

    - Submission on time: 0.2 pts

    - Initial post: 1 pt

    - Write 3 reactions 3 x0.3 = 0.9 pts

    - Get 3 reactions 3 x0.3 = 0.9 pts
