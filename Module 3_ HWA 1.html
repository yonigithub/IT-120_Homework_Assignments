    Please watch this video and reflect on the Human role in Cybersecurity:

    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=15lS2SfW4pM

    Write a reflection paper on the topic "Humans in Cybersecurity".

    Please follow the instructions:

    1. Reflection paper: 

    (1st page - title;

    2nd page - Abstract and Key Words;

    3rd page: first 1/2 page Introduction; rest of 1/2 page - Literature Review;

    4th page:  first1/2 page - Literature Review;  last 1/2 page - your reflection on the topic

    5th page: first1/2 page  -your reflection on the topic; rest of 1/2 page - Conclusions

    6th page: References (APA Format)

    For Literature Review - at least 3 references!

     

    2. Attach final work in Canvas as Word document;

    3. Use at least 2 visual aids (picture, graphics, etc) insert Figure/Table references and create a Table of Figures. Instructions here: How to insert Figure and Table references in Word documents (Links to an external site.)

    4. You must have at least 3 references in your paper AND you must use APA format for your references. Instructions are here: APA Referencing Guide and how to use the Word Referencing Tool (Links to an external site.)

    APA Format Explanation here: https://www.loom.com/share/01f20383295f47b9ba97235edaa3980e (Links to an external site.)

    Document format: https://www.siue.edu/~prose/classes/APAFormatTemplate.doc (Links to an external site.)

    Literature Review explanation: https://writingcenter.ashford.edu/writing-literature-review (Links to an external site.)

     

    For writing assistance - contact Marymount Center for Teaching and Learning (CTL) - they have writing tutors that can help you with writing.
